Who we are

The Radicubs Robotics Team is a community-based FRC team from Frisco, Texas. Our 25 students hail from 5 high schools across Frisco, representing our community through the FIRST Robotics Challenge and outreach.

Our purpose is to provide a free outlet for students of Frisco, Texas to pursue their interests in STEM and finance through the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC). Our mission is to create an environment where anyone can pursue and explore their interests in robotics and entrepreneurship and help build interest for our future generations by engaging in community outreach activities with them.

The Radicubs Robotics Team is recognized as a non-profit organization under a 501(c)(3).
